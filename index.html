Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?

Once your order is processed, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your shipment on our website.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. The item must be in its original condition. Please review our detailed return policy for more information.

Are there any discounts for students?

Yes, we offer student discounts. Present a valid student ID in-store or use your educational email address during online checkout to avail of the discount.

Are gift cards available for purchase?

Yes, we offer gift cards of various denominations. They can be purchased in-store or online and make for a perfect gift for any book lover.

Is there a book rental service available?

Currently, we do not offer a book rental service. However, you can explore our wide range of affordable book options for purchase.
